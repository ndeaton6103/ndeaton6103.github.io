The Seperatist Army

The Seperatist army consisted mostly of droids. These droids were led by the Kaleesh cyborg named General Grievous, and the army vastly outnumbered the clones. There were many different types of droids in this army, including: Command droids, Soldier droids, Super Battle droids, Droidekas, and many more.
