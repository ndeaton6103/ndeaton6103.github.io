The weapon of choice for all Force users.

The lightsaber was the weapon that all users of the force were known for carrying, an elegant laser sword that can be used to cut through anything and anyone. Listed below you will find the key components of these weapons.

Part Description
Energy Core: Used to power the blade
Focusing ring: Used to focus the power of the energy core into the kyber crystal
Kyber Crystal: A rare, Force-attuned crsytal that grew naturally on various planets across the galaxy, this crystal would give power to the wielder, as well as give the saber it's color
Blade emitter: Where the blade emits from the handle 
With these parts combined by the use of the Force, you will be promoted to be a Padawan
