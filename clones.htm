The Army of the Republic

The clones were the soldiers of the Republic's army during the Clone Wars. These soldiers were called clones because they were created using the DNA of Jango Fett. These soldiers were created for one purpose, follow orders. The clones were forced to listen to whateber commands they were given by the Chancellor, as well as the Jedi, this is what led to the Republic's downfall due to Order 66. This order was given to the clones by the Chancellor causing them to turn on their Jedi leaders.
